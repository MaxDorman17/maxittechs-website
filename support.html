Client support portal

Raise tickets, track progress, and manage your account in one place.

All existing managed contract clients have access to the MaxITTechs client portal at support.maxittechs.info. The portal is the correct and preferred channel for all support requests, including incidents, service requests, and general queries.

Using the portal ensures your request is logged, tracked against your SLA, and assigned to the appropriate engineer. Requests submitted via other channels may experience delays in acknowledgement and resolution.

Your login credentials were provided during onboarding. If you have lost access or have not yet received your credentials, contact your account manager.
