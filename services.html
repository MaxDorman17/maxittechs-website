Our Services

Managed IT support is our core service. We provide structured IT support packages for UK businesses alongside cybersecurity services, infrastructure projects and ongoing technology management.

Core service

Managed IT Support

Our managed IT services provide proactive monitoring, structured support and ongoing management of your technology environment under a predictable monthly cost.

Support is delivered through three service tiers: Essential, Professional and Enterprise, allowing businesses to choose the level of support that fits their operational needs.

  • Helpdesk support during business hours (Mon–Fri 08:30–17:30 UK)
  • Remote troubleshooting and scheduled support sessions
  • Proactive system monitoring and maintenance
  • Endpoint security and device management
  • Microsoft 365 administration and user lifecycle management
  • Patch management and security updates
  • Backup monitoring and restoration support
  • Infrastructure monitoring and alerting
  • Asset documentation and environment records
  • Strategic technology planning and reviews

Flexible managed IT support packages with clear per-user pricing.

Businesses can choose between Essential, Professional and Enterprise service tiers depending on their operational requirements.
