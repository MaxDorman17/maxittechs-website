Our services

Managed IT is our primary service. We also deliver IT projects, cybersecurity work, and limited ad-hoc support for existing clients and where capacity allows.

Core service

Managed IT Contracts

Our managed IT contracts provide structured, proactive support across defined areas of your technology environment under a fixed monthly fee with clearly documented SLAs. Coverage scope, device eligibility, and response commitments are confirmed in writing during onboarding.

  • 24/7 automated infrastructure monitoring with alerting
  • Patch management and software update scheduling
  • Helpdesk support during business hours — Mon–Fri 08:30–17:30 UK (phone, email, and portal)
  • Endpoint management across company-owned desktops and laptops on supported Windows versions
  • Network management including routers, switches, and Wi-Fi
  • Microsoft 365 Business Premium administration, user lifecycle management, and baseline security policy enforcement
  • Backup monitoring and routine restoration testing
  • Monthly reporting covering incidents, capacity, and recommendations
  • Quarterly strategic reviews with your account manager
  • Asset register and documentation maintained throughout the contract

All Managed IT agreements include a mandatory Microsoft 365 backup service to ensure business continuity and data recoverability.

One standardised per-user plan. Fixed monthly fee. 12-month agreement.

Project work

IT Projects

We plan and deliver one-off technology projects alongside or independent of a managed contract. Every project is scoped, costed, and delivered to an agreed specification.

Infrastructure & Networking

Server deployments, network redesigns, hardware refreshes, and data centre migrations. We survey, plan, and implement with minimal disruption to your operations.

  • Server installation and configuration
  • Structured cabling and network builds
  • Virtualisation and hypervisor deployments
  • Firewall and VPN configuration

Cloud Migration & Adoption

Moving workloads to Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365, or hybrid cloud environments with a clear migration plan, data integrity checks, and staff transition support.

  • Microsoft 365 tenant migration and setup
  • Azure infrastructure provisioning
  • On-premise to cloud server migrations
  • Hybrid identity and conditional access

Office & Site Relocations

Complete IT coordination for office moves — from decommissioning your current site to having your new location fully operational on day one.

  • Pre-move IT survey and planning
  • Structured cabling and patching at new site
  • Hardware relocation and reinstallation
  • Connectivity commissioning and testing

Technology Rollouts

Coordinated deployment of new devices, software platforms, or communication tools across your organisation, with documentation and user guidance included.

  • Device provisioning and Autopilot enrolment
  • Application packaging and deployment
  • Telephony and Teams Voice rollout
  • End-user handover documentation
Security services

Cybersecurity & Resilience

We approach security as a continuous discipline, not a one-time project. Our cybersecurity services combine preventative controls, automated monitoring and detection, and resilience planning to protect your business from the threats most likely to affect UK organisations.

  • Cyber security health checks and risk assessments
  • Endpoint detection and response (EDR) deployment
  • Email security — anti-phishing, anti-spoofing, and filtering
  • Multi-factor authentication rollout and enforcement
  • Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus support
  • Backup architecture review and implementation
  • Disaster recovery planning and tabletop exercises
  • Security awareness training for staff
  • Privileged access review and remediation
  • Incident response planning and documentation

Break/Fix Support

Break/fix work is available to existing managed contract clients only, subject to engineer availability. This is not a standalone service offering. Work is scoped and charged separately at an agreed day rate.

Remote Support

Fast, remote desktop access to diagnose and resolve issues on workstations, servers, or cloud platforms without the delay of an onsite visit.

  • Same-day remote sessions where available
  • Windows, macOS, and Linux support
  • Microsoft 365 and cloud platform issues
  • Clear resolution notes provided after each session

On-site Engineer Visits

An engineer dispatched to your premises to resolve hardware faults, carry out installations, or diagnose issues that cannot be addressed remotely.

  • Hardware fault diagnosis and resolution
  • Network troubleshooting and remediation
  • Printer, peripherals, and device setup
  • Post-visit summary and recommendations provided

Hardware Procurement

We source and supply business-grade hardware from reputable manufacturers, configured to your requirements and ready to deploy on delivery.

  • Laptops, desktops, and workstations
  • Networking equipment and servers
  • Pre-configuration and imaging
  • Warranty and replacement coordination

Emergency Call-Outs

For business-critical failures outside standard working hours, our emergency call-out service provides a best-effort response for contracted engagements. Availability is not guaranteed and is subject to engineer capacity.

  • Prioritised response for business-critical failures
  • Available outside standard business hours
  • Remote and onsite response options
  • Incident report and root cause analysis provided
