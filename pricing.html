How support works

Support model

Standard support hours
Monday–Friday, 08:30–17:30 UK (excluding public holidays)
Out-of-hours
Emergency P1 incidents only. Non-emergency issues are handled on the next business day.
Automated monitoring
24/7. Continuous live human supervision is not provided.
Emergency incident (P1)
Full business outage, server failure affecting multiple users, confirmed security breach, or Microsoft 365 tenant compromise. Single-user issues, peripheral failures, and performance concerns are not classified as P1.
Optional extras

Add-ons

Extend your plan with additional infrastructure management or coverage outside standard hours.

Additional Server

£125 /mo per server

Automated monitoring and patch management for a physical server beyond the one included in Business and Complete plans. Covers monitoring, patching, backup verification, and alert escalation. Application-layer support is excluded.

Additional Azure VM

£125 /mo per VM

Automated monitoring and patch management for an Azure VM beyond the one included in Business and Complete plans. Covers monitoring, patching, backup verification, and alert escalation. Application-layer and workload support is excluded.

BYOD Limited Support

£15 /user/mo

Limited support for personally-owned devices accessing business applications. Covers business app configuration and connectivity only. Personal hardware faults and home networks are excluded.

Out-of-Hours Cover

From £12 /user/mo

Priority 1 emergency response coverage outside standard hours (Mon–Fri 08:30–17:30). Non-emergency issues are handled on the next business day. Pricing varies by required coverage window. Contact us for a tailored quote.

The BYOD add-on is optional and available on all plans. Support is limited to business application access — including Microsoft 365, Teams, and Outlook — and does not extend to personal device hardware, performance issues, home network configuration, or non-business software.

Service requirements

Service eligibility

All managed users must meet the following baseline requirements before onboarding. Environments not meeting these criteria may require remediation work prior to contract commencement.

  • Microsoft 365 Business Premium licensing required for all managed users
  • Company-owned devices only — personally-owned hardware is excluded unless the BYOD add-on is in place
  • Supported Windows versions only — end-of-life operating systems are not covered
  • All devices must be enrolled in the management platform prior to support commencement
  • Unsupported or non-compliant systems may be excluded from coverage at assessment

Eligibility is confirmed during the pre-contract scoping call. Remediation of non-compliant systems prior to onboarding may be quoted as a separate project engagement.
