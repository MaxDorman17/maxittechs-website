Optional extras

Add-ons

Extend your plan with additional infrastructure management or coverage outside standard hours.

Additional Server

£125 /mo per server

Add management for a physical server beyond the one included in Business and Complete plans. Covers monitoring, patching, backup verification, and incident response.

Additional Azure VM

£125 /mo per VM

Add management for an Azure virtual machine beyond the one included in Business and Complete plans. Covers monitoring, patching, backup verification, and configuration management.

BYOD Limited Support

£15 /user/mo

Limited support for personally-owned devices accessing business applications. Covers business app configuration and connectivity only. Personal hardware faults and home networks are excluded.

Out-of-Hours Cover

From £12 /user/mo

Extended support coverage outside standard hours (Mon–Fri 09:00–17:30). Pricing varies by required coverage window. Contact us for a tailored quote.

The BYOD add-on is optional and available on all plans. Support is limited to business application access — including Microsoft 365, Teams, and Outlook — and does not extend to personal device hardware, performance issues, home network configuration, or non-business software.
